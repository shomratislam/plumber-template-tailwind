ABOUT US

We are a professional plumbing company committed to quality service and customer satisfaction. With years of experience in residential and commercial plumbing, we ensure reliable, fast, and affordable solutions tailored to your needs.

Plumber Points Company

Plumber Point is your trusted partner for dependable plumbing solutions. From minor leaks to major installations, we handle every job with care and expertise.

We’ve built our reputation on trust, transparency, and top-tier workmanship. Whether it’s your home or business, our licensed plumbers are equipped to fix, install, and maintain all types of systems — with the latest tools and a customer-first approach.

Residential Service

Expert plumbing support for houses, apartments, and villas—offering quick, efficient, and long-lasting solutions for everyday household plumbing challenges and upgrades..

Commercial Service

Custom plumbing services for offices, retail shops, and industrial units—ensuring smooth operation, minimal downtime, and compliance with commercial plumbing standards and safety codes.

Maintenance

Scheduled inspections and preventive measures designed to identify early plumbing problems, reduce unexpected failures, and extend the life of your plumbing systems effectively.

Emergency Repairs

Available day or night, our emergency team handles burst pipes, major leaks, and critical plumbing failures—restoring function and peace of mind immediately.

Installation Service

Seamless installation of plumbing fixtures including toilets, water heaters, and faucets—carried out with precision, care, and adherence to all building and safety regulations.

Drain Cleaning

Professional removal of clogs and buildup in sinks, toilets, and sewer lines—improving flow, preventing damage, and ensuring clean, odor-free plumbing performance.

Commercial Service

We provide expert plumbing solutions tailored to meet the demands of businesses, offices, and industrial spaces. From installations to maintenance, our team ensures minimal downtime, safety compliance, and efficient plumbing systems—keeping your operations smooth and your environment problem-free.
